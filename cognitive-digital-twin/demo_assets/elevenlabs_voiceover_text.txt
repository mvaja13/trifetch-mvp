===============================================================================
TIMING TIPS
===============================================================================

- Average speaking rate: 150 words per minute
- Intro segment: ~2 seconds (very short)
- Main segments: 8-12 seconds each
- Allow 0.5-1 second pause between sentences
- Total should be 45-60 seconds

If too fast: Adjust "Speed" slider in ElevenLabs (0.75x - 0.9x)
If too slow: Adjust "Speed" slider (1.1x - 1.25x)


===============================================================================
QUALITY CHECKS
===============================================================================

✓ Clear pronunciation (no mumbling)
✓ Natural pacing (not too fast/slow)
✓ Professional tone (not too casual)
✓ Consistent volume across segments
✓ No background noise
✓ Proper pauses between sentences


===============================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
===============================================================================

Problem: Voice sounds robotic
Solution: Reduce "Stability" to 40%, increase "Style" to 20%

Problem: Pronunciation wrong
Solution: Use phonetic spelling (e.g., "24 months" → "twenty-four months")

Problem: Audio too short
Solution: Add pauses with commas or periods

Problem: Audio too long
Solution: Remove filler words, use more concise language
